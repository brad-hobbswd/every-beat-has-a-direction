About the Book

Why was this book written, and why does music deserve serious biblical consideration?

Music has an extraordinary ability to shape the human heart. Long after conversations are forgotten and memories begin to fade, songs often remain. They accompany our celebrations, comfort our sorrows, influence our thinking, and quietly become part of the way we see the world.

Because music is so deeply connected to memory, emotion, and affection, it deserves careful biblical examination. This book is not an attempt to create a list of acceptable or unacceptable songs. Instead, it asks a more important question: What is the music we listen to forming within us?

Every Beat Has a Direction invites readers to consider how entertainment, worship, and discipleship intersect. Every song carries ideas, values, and desires. Whether intentionally or unintentionally, those messages influence the direction of the heart.

Why This Book?

To encourage believers to evaluate music through the lens of Scripture rather than personal preference.

Who Is It For?

For Christians who sincerely desire every area of life, including entertainment, to reflect their devotion to Jesus Christ.

What Will You Find?

Eight chapters that examine influence, worship, discernment, holiness, and the quiet power music has to shape the soul.

"The question is not simply what you're listening to. The question is what your listening is making of you."

Are You Ready?

Begin with the Introduction and walk through each chapter prayerfully and thoughtfully.
