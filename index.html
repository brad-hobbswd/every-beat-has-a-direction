About This Book

Music has always been more than entertainment. It shapes thoughts, influences emotions, forms memories, and quietly directs the heart. Every Beat Has a Direction invites readers to examine the voices they welcome into their lives and consider whether those voices are drawing them closer to Jesus Christ or further from His Kingdom.

Rather than focusing on rules or personal preferences, this book explores the biblical principles of worship, discipleship, discernment, and holiness, encouraging believers to surrender every area of life, including what they choose to hear.
