About Bradley Hobbs

Writing with a desire to encourage believers toward biblical truth, spiritual growth, and wholehearted devotion to Jesus Christ.

A Heart for Truth

Bradley Hobbs is a Christian author whose writing centers on Scripture, discipleship, spiritual growth, and living a life fully surrendered to Jesus Christ. His desire is to encourage readers to examine every area of life through the lens of God's Word while pursuing holiness, humility, and faithfulness.

Whether writing devotionals, biblical studies, or books that challenge readers to think deeply about everyday influences, Bradley's goal remains the same: to point hearts toward Christ and encourage believers to walk in truth.

Every Beat Has a Direction was written from the conviction that music is far more than entertainment. It shapes memory, influences worship, forms desires, and quietly helps direct the heart. This book invites readers to prayerfully examine those influences through Scripture.

"My prayer is not simply that you hear these words, but that every voice shaping your heart is measured against the voice of Christ."
